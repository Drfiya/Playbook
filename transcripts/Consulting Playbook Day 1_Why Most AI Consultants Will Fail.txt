The Four Readiness Checkpoints:
Before selling ANY implementation, ask:
1. Processes: Do they exist? Documented? If not, you're building on a garbage truck on fire
2. Data: Where does it live? SQL? Do they have a data dictionary? (Litmus test for maturity)
